What is Centralis?

Centralis is an application focused on the management/administration of a penitentiary center, that is, the staff, the inmates, and the facilities. Centralis allows you to do everything in a centralized manner from one place.

Features & Characteristics

The main features and characteristics are based on the addition, editing, and deletion of people (Inmates / Staff), in addition to the ability to interact with the facilities through the application's map and access both the center's schedules and the access records.

Why did we decide to create Centralis?

We decided to create this project because we saw a lack of systems dedicated to the management of a penitentiary center, so we thought it would be a good opportunity to bring a new application to a market not accustomed to having many options.
