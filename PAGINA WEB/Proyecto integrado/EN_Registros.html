Records:

The records will keep all the entries and exits of the inmates of the center, and will store the following data:

 • Type of record (entry/exit).
 • Record identifier.
 • Inmate identifier.
 • Reason for (entry/exit).
 • Date of the record.

This data will be essential for monitoring the inmates at all times.
