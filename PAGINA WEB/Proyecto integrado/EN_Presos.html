Prisoners:

The prisoners will hold a series of data, among them:

 • ID number.
 • Name.
 • Crime.
 • Cell.
 • A picture.

Among other data.

We will be able to perform different actions with each prisoner, such as:

 • Add a prisoner.
 • Edit a prisoner.
 • Delete a prisoner.

Additionally, each prisoner will be able to enter and exit the center under certain circumstances,
which will be recorded in the records.
